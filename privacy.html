Privacy policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our recycling information website collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is respected and that any information you provide is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This website is designed to provide recycling guidance for households, schools, and businesses.

Information we collect

We may collect basic personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details when you voluntarily submit them through forms or enquiries. We may also collect non-personal information such as browser type, device information, and general website usage data to help improve user experience and website performance.

How we use your information

Any information collected is used to respond to enquiries, improve our recycling resources, and enhance the overall functionality of the website. We may also use aggregated data to understand how users interact with our content so we can continue to improve educational recycling information for schools, businesses, and homeowners.

Data protection and security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure. While no method of online transmission is completely secure, we aim to follow best practices to ensure that your data is stored safely and responsibly.

Cookies and website usage

This website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyse website traffic. Cookies help us understand how visitors use our recycling resources so we can improve content and navigation. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Third-party services

We may use third-party tools such as analytics services to better understand website usage. These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to any personal information we may hold about you, as well as request corrections or deletion of your data where applicable. If you have any concerns about how your information is handled, you are encouraged to contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in website functionality or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of the website indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the website’s contact page. We will respond to enquiries as soon as possible and ensure your concerns are addressed appropriately.
