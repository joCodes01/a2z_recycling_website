Projects and partnerships

Working together

Our Projects and Partnerships page highlights the collaborative programs, sustainability initiatives, and community projects that support better recycling practices across homes, schools, and businesses. Through partnerships with local organisations, educational institutions, and industry leaders, we continue to provide reliable recycling information and encourage environmentally responsible waste management practices throughout the community.

  • Community recycling projects focused on waste reduction and sustainability awareness
  • Educational partnerships with schools and learning institutions
  • Business recycling support programs and sustainability initiatives
  • Local environmental campaigns and recycling awareness projects
  • Collaborative workshops and community engagement activities
  • Recycling education resources for households, schools, and businesses
  • Sustainability partnerships with environmental organisations
  • Ongoing projects promoting responsible waste management practices

Community Recycling Initiative Encourages Local Environmental Action

Our latest community recycling initiative focuses on increasing public awareness about correct recycling practices and reducing contamination in household recycling bins. Working alongside local councils and environmental groups, this project delivers educational workshops, practical recycling guides, and community clean-up events designed to encourage sustainable habits. The initiative aims to strengthen community participation while helping residents make more informed decisions about waste disposal and recycling management.

School Sustainability Partnership Expands Recycling Education

We are proud to partner with local schools to provide recycling education resources that support classroom learning and student engagement. This partnership includes interactive activities, educational materials, and sustainability projects that teach students the importance of responsible waste management and environmental protection. By encouraging recycling awareness at an early age, schools can help students develop long-term sustainable habits that positively impact future communities.

Business Recycling Collaboration Supports Workplace Sustainability

Our business recycling collaboration program helps organisations improve workplace recycling systems and reduce unnecessary waste. Participating businesses receive access to recycling information, sustainability resources, and practical strategies for improving waste sorting and disposal practices. Through ongoing partnerships with local businesses, this project supports environmentally responsible operations while encouraging stronger community involvement in sustainability and recycling initiatives.
