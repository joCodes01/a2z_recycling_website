Educational resources

Activities and socials

Our Educational Resources page provides schools, teachers, and students with valuable recycling information and sustainability learning materials designed to support environmental education. These resources help students understand the importance of waste reduction, responsible recycling practices, and protecting the environment through everyday actions. By making recycling education accessible and engaging, schools can encourage long-term sustainable habits both inside and outside the classroom.

  • Classroom recycling guides and sustainability lesson materials
  • Interactive environmental activities for primary and secondary students
  • Downloadable worksheets, posters, and recycling fact sheets
  • School recycling program support and waste reduction strategies
  • Educational videos and learning resources about recycling systems
  • Sustainability project ideas for classrooms and school events
  • Teacher support materials for environmental awareness education
  • Student-focused guides for improving recycling habits at school and home

New Classroom Recycling Activities Encourage Student Participation

Our latest classroom recycling activities are designed to help students better understand how recycling systems work and why waste reduction is important for the environment. These interactive learning resources include sorting activities, sustainability games, and practical recycling exercises that encourage hands-on learning within the classroom. By engaging students in environmental education, schools can help build positive recycling habits that support cleaner and more sustainable communities.

School Sustainability Programs Support Environmental Awareness

Schools participating in sustainability programs gain access to educational recycling resources, waste reduction strategies, and environmental awareness materials tailored for students and teachers. These programs encourage schools to create sustainable learning environments while helping students understand the impact of responsible waste management. Through classroom discussions, school recycling initiatives, and environmental projects, students are encouraged to become active participants in protecting the environment.

Teacher Resource Hub Expands Access to Recycling Education Materials

Our teacher resource hub provides easy access to recycling lesson plans, classroom posters, activity sheets, and sustainability learning guides that support environmental education across different year levels. These resources are designed to simplify recycling concepts for students while helping teachers deliver engaging and informative lessons about sustainability and waste management. By supporting environmental learning in schools, these educational materials encourage long-term awareness and responsible recycling behaviour among students.
